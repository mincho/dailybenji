Questions

Do you connect to my bank?

No. We never ask for your bank login and don't wire into your accounts through any third-party app. You forward your bank's transaction-alert emails — those don't contain credentials or account numbers.

What banks work?

14+ major banks out of the box — Chase, Amex, Capital One, Citi, Bank of America, Discover, Wells Fargo, and more — plus a generic profile for anything else, and manual or CSV entry for the rest.

Can I share it with my partner?

Yes. Add a collaborator — a spouse, partner, or accountant — and you'll share one account. You can also CC additional people on the daily email.

What happens if I cancel?

Cancel anytime from settings. When you do, every transaction, memo, tag, and goal is permanently deleted — we show you the count before you confirm.

Can I get my data out?

Anytime. Export a full CSV, or a ZIP that bundles the CSV with every receipt you've attached — filtered however you like. Great for tax season, expense reporting, or securely sharing with your favorite AI tool or LLM.
