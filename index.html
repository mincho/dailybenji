What You Spent, In Your Inbox. Every Morning.

Welcome to The Daily Benji — a no-fluff, no-login, no-bull newsletter that helps you make sense of your spending. It’s like the daily business section of your life, minus the ads for luxury watches.

How It Works

  • You auto-forward your spend alerts to your personal dailybenji inbox.
  • Benji parses and summarizes spending.
  • Every morning, get a single plain-text email with what you spent and spending insights
  • And the Sunday edition shows you a comprehensive summary of your spend activity
  • No apps. No logins. Secure and private. Just email.
