Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Daily Benji

Getting Started

How does Daily Benji work?

You forward your bank transaction emails to your unique Daily Benji address. We organize them and send you a beautiful summary every morning at.

What banks do you support?

All major US banks and credit cards that send transaction emails, including Chase, Bank of America, Citi, Capital One, Amex, Wells Fargo, Discover, and many more.

How long does setup take?

About 15 minutes total. You'll spend 3-5 minutes per credit card enabling alerts, then a few minutes setting up email forwarding rules.

Do you need my bank password?

Never. We only read the transaction emails you forward to us. We never access your bank accounts or require any passwords.

Using Daily Benji

What time does the email arrive?

Whenever you want! But your Daily Benji is set to arrive at 6 AM in your timezone, every single day. You can change this time by replying to any daily email.

What if I get a new credit card?

Just turn on transaction alerts for the new card and add its email address to your forwarding rules. Takes about 5 minutes.

Can I track cash purchases?

Not currently. Daily Benji tracks credit and debit card transactions that generate email alerts.

Can my spouse get the emails too?

Yes! Add their email as a "reporting email" in your settings, and they'll receive the same daily summary.

Privacy & Security

What data do you store?

We only store transaction details from forwarded emails: merchant names, amounts, dates, and the last 4 digits of your card. We never see full card numbers or account details.

Do you sell my data?

Never. You pay us a subscription, we provide a service. Your spending data is never sold, shared, or used for marketing.

How do I delete my data?

Email us at support@dailybenji.com and we'll remove all your data within 24 hours. We'll confirm once it's done.

Account & Billing

How much does it cost?

Daily Benji is free during our beta period. When we launch, it will be a simple monthly subscription (beta users get a discount).

How do I cancel?

You can cancel anytime from your account dashboard. Simple and straightforward, no hoops to jump through.

Can I change my magic email address?

Not currently. Your magic email is permanent once created, so choose wisely! This helps ensure reliable email delivery.
