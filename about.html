Why We Built This

Here's the truth: between the two of us, we have over 20 credit cards. We tried every app. They all had the same problem: we'd set them up, feel virtuous for a week, then never open them again.

The login would time out. Two-factor codes would expire. By the time we actually got in, we'd forgotten why we opened the app. Checking our spending became a quarterly guilt trip instead of a daily habit.

So we built Daily Benji for ourselves. One email, every morning, with yesterday's damage. No logging in. No categories to manage. No graphs to interpret. Just the facts about where our money went, delivered with our morning coffee.

"Financial success isn't about complex budgets or fancy tools. It's about awareness. When you see your spending every single day, you naturally spend less. Those extra $50-100 a month? That's real money for retirement, college funds, or finally taking that vacation."
