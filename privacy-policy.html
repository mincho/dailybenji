Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

The Daily Benji ("Daily Benji," "we," "us") is an email-based spending tracker available only to people who reside in the United States. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. We've kept it in plain English.

The short version

  • We never connect to your bank or ask for your bank login.
  • We only see the transaction-alert emails you choose to forward, plus the account details you give us.
  • We don't sell your data, run ads, or use third-party advertising trackers.
  • You can export or delete your data anytime. Cancel, and we erase everything.
  • Daily Benji is available only to US residents.

1. What we collect

Information you give us

  • Your name and email address
  • Your time zone (so summaries arrive at the right local time)
  • Any additional email addresses you ask us to send summaries to
  • Your account settings, tags, memos, and support messages

Spending data you send us

The transaction-alert emails you forward, plus anything you enter manually or import by CSV. From a forwarded alert we extract only:

  • The merchant name
  • The amount and date
  • The card name or nickname your bank includes
  • Sometimes the last four digits of the card

Payment information

Payments are handled by Stripe. Your card details go directly to Stripe over an encrypted connection — we never receive or store your full card number. Stripe returns only limited details (such as the last four digits, expiration date, and billing ZIP) so we can show your payment method and send renewal reminders.

Basic technical data

Like any website, our servers keep standard logs (such as IP address, browser type, and timestamps) needed to operate and secure the service, and we use essential cookies to keep you signed in.

2. What we never collect or access

  • Your bank login or password — we have no bank connection and use no aggregator (no Plaid, Yodlee, or similar)
  • Full bank account or card numbers
  • Your account balances
  • Social Security, tax ID, or driver's license numbers

3. How we use your information

  • Generate your daily summaries and the spending views in your dashboard
  • Send the daily email to you and any recipients you authorize
  • Process your subscription and send billing and service notices
  • Provide support and respond to your messages
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and meet our legal obligations

We do not use your data for advertising, and we never sell or rent it.

4. How your information is shared

We share data only with the service providers that help us run Daily Benji, under agreements that limit them to that purpose:

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Postmark — sending email
  • Render — hosting

We may also disclose information if required by law (see section 9). We never sell your personal information or share it for advertising.

5. How long we keep it

  • Forwarded emails: once we've parsed a forwarded alert into a transaction, we delete the original email automatically within 30 days.
  • Your transactions and account data: kept while your account is active so we can show your history.
  • After you cancel: permanently deleted within 3 business days (see section 7).

6. How we protect it

  • Encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
  • Hosted on reputable, secured infrastructure
  • Employee access to user data is limited and need-based

No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

7. Your rights and choices

  • Access & export: view all your transactions in your dashboard and export them as CSV, or a ZIP with receipts, anytime
  • Correct: edit your information and transactions whenever you like
  • Delete: cancel your account to erase your data
  • Recipients: add or remove additional email recipients in settings

When you cancel, we permanently delete your transaction history, memos, tags, goals, and related records within 3 business days. We show you what will be removed before you confirm. Deletion is permanent and can't be undone. We may keep limited billing records where the law requires it.

To exercise any of these, use your account at app.dailybenji.com or email support@dailybenji.com.

8. Cookies and tracking

We use only the essential cookies needed to keep you signed in and operate the site. We don't use third-party advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.

9. Legal requests

If we're legally required to disclose information, we provide only what's necessary, and we'll notify you unless the law prohibits it.

10. Children's privacy

Daily Benji isn't directed to anyone under 18, and we don't knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we'll delete it.

11. California residents

If you're a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, to request its deletion, and not to be discriminated against for exercising those rights. We do not sell or share your personal information as those terms are defined under California law. To make a request, email support@dailybenji.com.

12. United States only

Daily Benji is intended only for people who reside in the United States. We don't offer the service to, or knowingly collect information from, people outside the US, and your information is stored and processed in the United States. If you don't reside in the US, please don't use Daily Benji.

13. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we'll update the date at the top and notify you by email or with a notice on the site.

14. Contact us
