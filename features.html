Features

Simple to read. Powerful to use.

The daily email is the heart of Daily Benji. Underneath is a full app you own and control. Here's everything it does.

The daily email

One briefing every morning, at the hour and time zone you choose.

  • Yesterday at a glance. The headline number, transaction count, and how it compares to your 30-day average.
  • Grouped by card. Every charge with its merchant, category, tag, and amount — totaled per account.
  • Anticipated spend. Recurring charges expected in the next five days, predicted from your own history.
  • Discretionary vs essential. See at a glance how much of yesterday was spending you actually control.
  • A different deep-dive daily. Category trends, top merchants, observations, goal coaching, and a full weekly review.
  • Reply to ask. Hit reply and ask in plain English — and CC a partner or accountant, too.

Getting your spending in

Three ways, whichever fits your life — no bank logins, ever.

  • Auto-forward bank alerts. One forwarding rule, set once. Works with 14+ major banks out of the box, plus a generic profile.
  • Manual entry. Single-page bulk add with autocomplete. Twenty transactions in a minute.
  • CSV import. Upload exports from your bank, card, or old tool — we remember the column mapping.
  • Multi-currency. 14+ currencies auto-convert to USD at daily rates, with the original amount preserved.
  • Duplicate prevention. De-duplicated on the email's message ID, so a double-forward never double-counts.
  • No bank logins. Alerts carry no credentials or account numbers — automation without the keys.

Make it yours

Organize spending the way you think about it.

  • Smart auto-categorization. Your personal rules, then community rules, then an AI fallback — remembered forever once set.
  • Tags & memos. Your own colored labels and free-text notes, used in filters, exports, and the daily email.
  • Receipts. Paste, drag, or upload photos and PDFs onto any transaction — up to five each.
  • Recurrence. Mark a charge monthly, annual, or one-time; the pattern auto-applies to similar charges.
  • Non-spending markers. Exclude transfers, card payments, and reimbursements — by transaction, merchant, or category.
  • Reminders. Set one on any charge; it surfaces in your daily email until done.

See it any way

The email is enough on its own — but the app is there when you want to dig in.

  • Dashboard. Month-to-date spend, daily average, projected month-end, and a 30-day sparkline.
  • All Activity. A searchable, filterable table with inline category editing and bulk updates.
  • By category, merchant, tag, or account. Totals, percentages, lifetime spend, and last activity.
  • Calendar heatmap. A month view of your spending days. Click any day to see exactly what happened.
  • Anticipated spend. The full list of predicted recurring charges, with confidence based on history.
  • Account management. Link a new card to an old one, merge duplicates, and rename accounts inline.

Insights & goals

Specific, dated patterns — never vague nudges. Every one cites real transactions.

  • Dated observations. Anomalies, category swings, day-of-week patterns, your largest day, spending pace.
  • Subscription watch. An active roll-up, price-change alerts, and "possibly canceled" flags.
  • Opportunity cost. "Sweetgreen ≈ $1,612/year — compounded over 10 years at 7%, ≈ $22,290." The math, not a lecture.
  • Goals, four ways. Cap spending overall, by category, merchant, or tag — over 7 days, 30 days, the month, or a custom window.
  • Goal coaching. On-pace / over-pace status, projected outcome, days remaining, and where to ease up.
  • Verify or dismiss. Each insight links to the transactions behind it. Dismiss anything you don't want to see again.
