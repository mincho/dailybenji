Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 12, 2025

Overview

The Daily Benji helps you track spending without accessing your bank accounts. We only see the transaction emails you forward to us—nothing more. This policy explains how we handle your information.

What We Collect

When You Sign Up

  • Your name and email address
  • Time zone (for accurate daily summaries)
  • Any additional email addresses you want to receive summaries

From Your Transaction Emails

We extract only what's needed:

  • Transaction amounts
  • Merchant names
  • Transaction dates
  • Bank provided card name or nickname

What We Never Access

  • Bank account credentials (we don't have access to your actual bank account)
  • Full credit card numbers (we don't have access to your full credit card numbers)
  • Social Security numbers (we don't have access to sensitive information like Social Security or Driver's license numbers)
  • Your actual bank balance (we don't have access to your bank)

Payment Processing

We use Stripe as our trusted, PCI-DSS-compliant payment processor to handle all subscription and billing transactions. When you enter your payment information on our site, it is sent directly to Stripe over a secure, encrypted connection.

The Daily Benji does not receive, store, or have access to your full credit card number, bank account number, or other sensitive payment details. Stripe processes your recurring subscription payments on our behalf and only returns limited, non-sensitive information to us — such as the last four digits of your card, expiration date, and billing zip code — so that we can display your payment method in your account settings and send renewal reminders.

How We Use Your Data

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Generate your daily spending summaries
  • Show spending trends compared to your own history
  • Send summaries to any email addresses you've authorized
  • Process your subscription
  • Provide customer support

We never sell your data or use it for advertising.

Data Storage & Security

Your Transaction Emails

  • Stored securely in our database
  • Viewable in your dashboard anytime
  • Only accessible by you
  • Incinerated automatically at most 15 days after processing

Security Measures

  • All connections are encrypted (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Servers hosted on secure infrastructure
  • Regular security updates
  • Limited employee access to user data

Service Providers

We work with trusted partners:

  • Stripe - Payment processing
  • Postmark - Email delivery
  • Render.io - Server hosting

Your Rights

You control your data:

  • Export - Download your transactions as a CSV file
  • Update - Change your information anytime
  • Delete - Cancel your account and we'll delete your data within 3 business days
  • Access - View all your transaction emails in your dashboard

Contact support@dailybenji.com or visit app.dailybenji.com to exercise these rights.

Email Communications

What We Send

  • Daily spending summaries (to you and any emails you've added)
  • Essential service updates
  • Billing notifications

What We Don't Send

  • Marketing emails
  • Third-party promotions
  • Your data to anyone else

Legal Compliance

Data Requests

If legally required to disclose information:

  • We provide only what's legally necessary
  • We notify you unless prohibited by law

California Residents

Under CCPA, you have the right to:

  • Know what data we collect
  • Delete your personal information
  • Opt-out of data sales (we don't sell data)

Data Deletion

When you cancel your account:

  • Your data is permanently deleted within 3 business days
  • Deletion includes all transaction history and emails
  • This action cannot be undone

Updates to This Policy

If we make significant changes:

  • We'll email you
  • We'll post a notice on our website
  • Your continued use means you accept the updates

Contact Us

Questions about privacy?

Email: support@dailybenji.com

Our Commitment We built The Daily Benji because tracking spending shouldn't mean giving up your privacy. We only see what you explicitly share with us, and we guard that information carefully. Your financial data stays yours.
