1

Enable Transaction Alerts

Do this once for each credit card you own

Every bank can email you when you make a purchase. They just don't turn it on by default. Here's what to do:

üí≥ For each of your credit cards:
  1. Log into your bank's website (not the app)
  2. Find "Alerts" or "Notifications" in settings
  3. Turn on email alerts for all transactions over $0
  4. Make sure they're sent to your primary email
üéØ Pro Tip

Set the threshold to $0.01 to catch every transaction. Some banks default to $50+ which misses most daily spending.
