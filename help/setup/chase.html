Chase Setup

Enable transaction alerts so Daily Benji can track your spending

Using the Chase Mobile App (Recommended)

  1. Log in to the Chase Mobile app.
  2. Tap on the profile icon in the top-right corner.
  3. Under "Alerts & messages", select "Manage alerts".
  4. Choose the credit card account you want to set up.
  5. Go to the "Transactions" or "Security alerts" section.
  6. To get alerts for every purchase, set a transaction amount threshold of $0.01.
  7. Select your delivery method: Email.
  8. Save your changes.

Using the Chase Website

  1. Sign in to your Chase account at chase.com.
  2. Navigate to the "Profile & settings" section (may be under "Account management").
  3. Select "Alerts".
  4. Click on "Choose alerts" and select your credit card account.
  5. Customize your alert preferences, ensuring you select Email as the delivery method for transactions. Set the amount to $0.01.
  6. Click "Save" to enroll.

Important

For Daily Benji to work, you must select Email as the delivery method for your transaction alerts.
