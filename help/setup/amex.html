American Express Setup

Enable transaction alerts so Daily Benji can track your spending

Using the American Express Website

  1. Log in to your account at americanexpress.com
  2. Click on "Account Management" in the navigation bar
  3. Find and select "Card Alerts" (often under "Alerts, Communications & Privacy")
  4. Choose the types of alerts you want to receive. Select Purchase Alerts
  5. Set alerts for all purchases, or for purchases over $0.01
  6. Choose Email as your delivery method
  7. Save your preferences

Using the Amex Mobile App

  1. Log in to the Amex mobile app
  2. Select the card you wish to manage and tap the "Account" tab
  3. Tap on "Notifications"
  4. Enable alerts for purchases. Note: The app focuses on push notifications, so use the website method above for reliable email alerts

Important

For Daily Benji to work, you must select Email as the delivery method for your transaction alerts. The website method is more reliable for setting up email alerts than the mobile app.
