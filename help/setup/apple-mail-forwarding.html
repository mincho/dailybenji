iCloud Email Forwarding Setup

Set up automatic forwarding from your iCloud email account to your Daily Benji inbox.

For iCloud Email

  1. Go to icloud.com/mail in your web browser and sign in
  2. Click the gear icon at the top of the Mailboxes list
  3. Choose "Settings" from the dropdown menu
  4. Click "Rules" in the settings menu
  5. Click "Add a Rule"
  6. Name your rule (e.g., "Forward Bank Alerts")

  7. Select your bank(s) to generate filter strings:

    American Express
    Capital One
    Chase
    Barclays
    Citi
    Discover
    USAA

    From Filter String:

    Subject Filter String:

  8. Under "If a message", select "is from"
  9. Paste your bank email string in the text field
  10. Click "Add another condition"
  11. Under "If a message", select "Subject includes"
  12. Paste your subject filter string in the text field
  13. Under "Then", select "Forward to"
  14. Enter your Daily Benji email address
  15. Click "Done"

Important Notes

Ensure you are logged into the correct iCloud account when setting up forwarding.
